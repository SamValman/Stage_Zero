This is currently a placeholder website to provide a brief description of what Stage Zero is, how it has been developed, and to start collating links for available resources. A more extensive description of Stage Zero, completed projects, and planned restoration works will be included in the website under development. If you are a practitioner or researcher in this field and have resources or wish to be identified on this final webpage please email samuel.valman@nottingham.ac.uk.

Resources

All resources referenced throughout the site can be found here along with further readings and websites. It is recommended to first watch the Webinars to hear an overview of the Stage Zero process goal diretly from those who have been most involved with it for the longest.
