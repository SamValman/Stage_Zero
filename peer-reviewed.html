Bibliography of academic articles

Here we provide a bibliography of academic articles that would be useful if you wish to learn more about Stage Zero and it's possible benefits. Many are from parallel fields such as wetland restoration and are included to provide the evidence and research which has been built on to understand Stage Zero and its impact on the landscapes. Click on any article to be taken to the source.
